Container & Equipment Repositioning

Our Method

Utilizing our extensive network of flatbed and specialty trailer carriers , we can provide unmatched one-way or round-trip equipment repositioning. The use of flatbeds in order to accomplish equipment repositioning gives shippers access to a larger carrier base at competitive rates.

Credentials

We have access to every major port on all 3 US coasts and are active members of the UIIA, simplifying many movements that might otherwise be complicated and cause the shipper or driver to spend more time on a load than needed. Let our experience be your asset.

Container Repositioning

We have extensive experience repositioning every type of container. Whether you need to move a 10' container, two 40' flat rack units, or two 20' containers, we can move the units to any port, rail yard, or off-dock facility. As long as a lift is present to unload the flatbed, we will reposition the unit quickly, safely, and efficiently.

Chassis Repositioning

As a natural evolution from hauling containers on flatbed trailers, we also reposition container chassis. Waiting to find a power-only unit willing to move a one-way load is too time consuming. By repositioning chassis on flatbeds, you can avoid that headache and move up to four 40' chassis or six 20' chassis per flatbed.

Genset Repositioning

In order to provide a full repositioning service to equipment providers and ocean liners, we also reposition generator sets or "gensets". Typically, we can move anywhere from one to 18 gensets on a flatbed, depending on type. Whether they're headed to a port, rail yard, or other facility, we will get them there efficiently.
